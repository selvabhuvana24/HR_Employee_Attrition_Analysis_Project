# HR Employee Attrition Analysis

# Project Overview
Employee attrition is one of the major challenges faced by organizations, as high turnover affects productivity,
increases recruitment costs, and impacts overall employee morale. This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition data to
identify the key factors contributing to employee turnover and provide meaningful business insights.Using Python data analysis
and visualization techniques, the project explores how factors such as department, salary, experience, overtime, job satisfaction,
and work-life balance influence employee attrition.

# Objective
The main objective of this project is to:
- Analyze employee attrition patterns
- Identify departments with high turnover
- Understand the impact of salary and experience on attrition
- Examine employee satisfaction and work-life balance
- Generate business insights using visualizations
- Provide recommendations to improve employee retention

# Key Insights
- Employees working overtime showed higher attrition rates.
- Lower salary groups experienced greater employee turnover.
- Poor work-life balance contributed significantly to attrition.
- Employees with lower job satisfaction were more likely to leave.
- Certain departments showed comparatively higher attrition rates.

# Recommendations
- Reduce excessive overtime policies
- Improve employee engagement initiatives
- Introduce better salary and incentive programs
- Enhance work-life balance strategies
- Focus on employee satisfaction and retention programs
